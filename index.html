Portal Solutions are used in over 10M companies for their daily use and all their business needs.

What are the Portals?

Customer Portal

A portal where data of all the customers carrying out business with an organization is maintained.

Vendor Portal

A portal where data of all the vendors carrying out business with an organization is maintained.

Employee Portal

A portal to have quick and easy access to HR-related transactions and services.

Maintenance Portal

A portal to view, edit and create notifications and work orders, integrated with Firebase Cloud Messaging.

Shop Floor Portal

A portal used for maintaining the data of all the production orders and planned orders carried out in a shop floor of an enterprise.

EHSM Portal

A portal used for viewing, editing and creating new Incidents in the plant and for Risk Assessment in the enterprise.

Quality Portal

A portal used for maintaining the data of the inspection lots, result recording and usage decision of the lot carried out by the Quality Engineer of an organization.
